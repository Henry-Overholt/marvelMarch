Marvel March is the month when we celebrate the Marvel Cinematic Universe, by watching all the Marvel Movies in what ever order you want.

Above are two different orders, one is the chronological order based on when each movie takes place. The other is my variation of when the movies were released. Some of the order is flipped based on my opinion of when you should watch them.

I've built this as something to do while being self-isolated during the Coronavirus outbreak, but that shouldn't stop you from using this resource to watch anytime. It doesn't have to be March to watch all the Marvel movies, it can be any month. Although it won't have the alliteration of Marvel March, it will still be just as fun.

Since Disney owns Marvel most can be found on Disney +, others can be found on different streaming services, but all will require the necessary subscriptions.
